Easy and Quick Halushki

A well easily and quickly cooked halushki

Ingredients

  • 1 pound bacon
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 1 (16 ounce) package egg noodles
  • 1 head cabbage, sliced
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Snip bacon into small pieces with a scissors and cook in a large skillet
    over medium heat until crisp, stirring often, about 10 minutes
  2. Cook and stir onion with bacon until translucent, about 5 more minutes
  3. Set bacon and onion aside, leaving drippings in the skillet
  4. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil
  5. Cook egg noodles in the boiling water
  6. Stirring occasionally until cooked through but firm to the bite, about 5 minutes
  7. Drain
  8. Transfer bacon and onion mixture with drippings into the pot used to cook
    noodles and cook and stir cabbage until coated with drippings
  9. Cover pot and cook until cabbage is tender, 10 to 12 minutes, stirring occasionally
  10. Gently stir in noodles and season to taste with salt and black pepper
  11. Serve and enjoy!
